    • How to play

    Industrialist simulator is an incremental clicker game. There are a few stats to keep up with.

  • GDP - This is your main currency used to purchase items. Your goal is to get it as high as possible.

  • Happiness - This acts as a multiplier, some items increase this. When your happiness goes up, you earn more.

  • Citizens - This is your population. You need citizens for most items, different items cost different amounts. If you run out of citizens you need to buy more to purchase things like workers or factories.

  • Food - You need food for your population to work. Without food, your workers will stop working. You can get food with things like garderns or farms. If you aren't earning money, you probably aren't getting enough food.
